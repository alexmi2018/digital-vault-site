Data deletion

Digital Vault Data Deletion

Digital Vault does not create a developer-hosted account. Your vault data is stored locally on your device.

You can delete your local Digital Vault data by clearing the app data in Android settings or by uninstalling the app.

Google Drive backup is optional and controlled by you. If you have saved backup files to your Google Drive, you can delete those files directly from your Google Drive.

For help with support-related data or Pro purchase support, contact digitalvault.app@gmail.com.

Some purchase records may be handled by Google Play according to Google Play policies.
