Android password and records vault

Keep passwords and private records on your device.

Digital Vault is a secure local password and private records vault for Android. Your data is stored locally in encrypted form, and backup and restore with Google Drive is available as an optional feature you control.

Read the Privacy Policy Get Support

Local by design

Vault data is saved on your device, not stored in a developer account. The app is designed for everyday use on Android.

Encrypted backup and restore

If you choose to use Google Drive backup and restore, your backup file is encrypted before upload and can be restored later with your app access.

Optional Pro features

Google Play Billing may be used for Pro features when enabled. You can review purchase support details on the support page.
