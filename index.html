Android password and records vault

Your private vault stays on your device.

Digital Vault is an Android-first vault for passwords, private records, and recovery notes. Your data is stored locally in encrypted form, and optional encrypted Google Drive backup and restore is available when you want an extra copy you control.

No server vault: your vault content stays on the device unless you choose encrypted backup.

Product overview

Built for everyday vault use on Android.

Simple tools for storing, checking, importing, and restoring private information without pushing your vault into a server account.

Local encrypted vault

Vault data stays on your device in encrypted form and is unlocked with your master password.

Passwords and private records

Store passwords, notes, and other private records in a few clear templates that stay easy to find.

Password generator

Create strong passwords when you need a new account or want to replace an old one.

2FA / TOTP codes

Keep one-time codes together with the entries they belong to for quicker sign-in.

Android Autofill service

Fill usernames and passwords directly into fields in other apps and websites using Android's system autofill.

Optional encrypted Google Drive backup

Back up and restore your vault with an encrypted file that is uploaded only when you choose it.

Password health and security insights

Review your vault for weak, reused, or outdated passwords and see simple security guidance.

Duplicate cleanup

Find repeated entries and clean up clutter so your vault stays organized.

Safe Google Password Manager CSV import

Import supported CSV exports with care so you can move data into Digital Vault more safely.

Optional Pro features when available

Some Pro features may be offered through Google Play Billing if they are enabled later.

Built for Android first

Digital Vault is designed around Android phones and tablets. If desktop or other builds are offered later, they will be treated as separate support targets rather than the main public release.
