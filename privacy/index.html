Privacy Policy for Digital Vault

Last updated: June 9, 2026

Digital Vault is designed to store vault data locally on your device. In normal use, your vault stays on your phone unless you choose to use a backup feature.

What the app stores

The app stores your vault content on your device in encrypted form. Your master password is not sent to the developer. Vault content is not collected by the developer.

Google Drive backup and restore

Google Drive backup and restore is optional and controlled by you. If you choose to use it, the backup file is encrypted before it is uploaded to your Google Drive. You can remove backup files from your Google Drive at any time.

Google services and billing

The app may rely on Google services that are required by Android, including Google Play services, Google Drive sign-in and backup, and Google Play Billing when Pro features are enabled.

Your choices

You can delete local app data by uninstalling the app or by clearing the app's data in Android settings. You can delete Google Drive backup files from your Google Drive.

Support

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@example.com.
