Support

Digital Vault Support

For help, contact digitalvault.app@gmail.com.

1. Contact support

Send a short description of the issue, what you were trying to do, and any details that help us reproduce it.

2. What to include in a support request

Please include your phone model, Android version, app version if you know it, what you were doing, what went wrong, and screenshots if possible.

3. Backup and restore help

If you use Google Drive backup, make sure you are signed in to the same Google account and allow the app to access Drive when prompted. Backup and restore work only when you start them from inside the app.

4. Google Drive account access

If Drive access fails, check that the Google account on the device is the one you want to use and that Google Drive is available on the device. You can try signing in again if the app prompts you.

5. Deleting local data

To remove local data, uninstall the app or clear the app's data in Android settings.

6. Deleting Google Drive backup files

If you use Google Drive cloud backup, encrypted backup files may be stored in a hidden app-specific area of your Google Drive account associated with Digital Vault. To delete that cloud backup data, open Google Drive settings, go to Manage apps, find Digital Vault, open its options, and delete its hidden app data.

7. Pro features and purchase support

If you need help with a Pro feature or a purchase, include the email address used for the purchase and a short description of the issue when you contact support.

8. Closed testing feedback

If you are using a closed or internal test build, please include your phone model, Android version, app version if known, what you were doing, what went wrong, and screenshots if possible.
